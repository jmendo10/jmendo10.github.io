Website Evaluations

Good Website Layout

Spotify is a music streaming service used all around the world.

  1. C:Contrast - Spotify complements well and guides the user.
  2. R:Repetition - Spotify uses similar patterns throughout.
  3. A:Alignment - This has important links organized using the left-to-right principle for better reading.
  4. P:Proximity - Kept together and offered guidance.

Bad website Layout

The Big ugly Website is just what it says in the name as a unorganized unappealing mess.

  1. C:Contrast - This doesnt complement well and is an eyesore.
  2. R:Repetition - From the words to images and even text it seems sparatic and not administered well.
  3. A:Alignment - Alignment here is poor and has some parts overlapping. Everything is focused on the left as well.
  4. P:Proximity - The work here is jumbled together causing overlap and sometimes too close to distinguih diffrent parts.
